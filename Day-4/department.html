DEPARTMENT OF POST GRADUATION

ABOUT

D.N.R. College in Bhimavaram, Andhra Pradesh, offers a range of postgraduate (PG) courses, primarily in fields like science, social sciences, arts, commerce, and management. The PG departments follow the guidelines of Adikavi Nannaya University and Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education, using a semester system for examinations.

Key aspects of DNR College's PG Department:
Courses Offerings:

DNR College offers a variety of postgraduate courses, including M.Sc., MCA, and MBA/PGDM

Admissions:

DNR College admissions are typically based on entrance exams like AUCET/ICET, with additional criteria set by the university.

Evaluation:

The college uses a semester system with internal and external assessments for all subjects, except for foundation courses and practical exams.

Academic Objectives:

DNR College aims to be a center of excellence for human resource development in diverse fields, focusing on community-oriented and learner-centric approaches.

Results:

Examination results are displayed on the college website, and students can apply for revaluation.
